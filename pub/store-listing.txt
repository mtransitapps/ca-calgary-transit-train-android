Full description:
This app adds Calgary Transit C-Trains information to MonTransit.

This app provides the schedule as well as news from @calgarytransit on Twitter and YouTube.

Calgary Transit C-Trains serve Calgary in Alberta, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display C-Trains information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Calgary Transit and the City of Calgary.
https://www.calgarytransit.com/developer-resources

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-calgary-transit-train-android

This app is not related with Calgary Transit and the City of Calgary.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from Twitter and YouTube
